Why This Matters

Waterborne Diseases Kill Millions Every Year

Contaminated water causes diseases like Cholera, Typhoid, and Dysentery. Early detection and rapid response can save thousands of lives.

🦠

3.4 Million Deaths Annually

WHO estimates that waterborne diseases cause over 3.4 million deaths per year, mostly in developing regions. Early surveillance is critical.

🚰

2 Billion People at Risk

Over 2 billion people worldwide use drinking water sources contaminated with fecal matter. Continuous monitoring is essential.

⏱️

80% Preventable

Most waterborne disease outbreaks are preventable with timely water quality testing, AI-based risk prediction, and immediate community alerts.

Platform Features

Everything You Need to Safeguard Water Quality

Our integrated platform combines real-time data collection, machine learning, and instant communication to create a comprehensive defense against waterborne diseases.

🧪

Water Sample Collection

Field officers can submit water quality data (pH, Turbidity, Nitrates, E.coli) from any location with automatic geocoding for Tamil Nadu districts and villages.

🤖

AI-Powered Risk Prediction

Our machine learning engine uses Logistic Regression to calculate contamination indexes and predict outbreak probability in real-time with every sample submission.

📊

Interactive Dashboard

Visualize trends with monthly case charts, district comparisons, water source breakdowns, and risk distribution — all updating live as new data flows in.

🚨

Instant SMS & WhatsApp Alerts

When contamination risk exceeds 70%, the system automatically generates alerts and enables one-click SMS/WhatsApp notifications to affected communities.

💡

Smart Insights Engine

For every contaminated sample, the AI generates the likely cause, potential health effects, and immediate remedies — actionable information for field teams.

👥

Role-Based Access Control

Three-tier hierarchy: Field Officers collect data, District Admins oversee alerts, and State Admins manage the entire surveillance network.

How It Works

From Sample to Action in 4 Simple Steps

Our streamlined workflow ensures rapid response to any water quality threat.

1

Collect Sample

Field officers test water at the source and record pH, turbidity, nitrates, and E.coli readings.

2

Submit Data

Enter readings via the web portal. Location and coordinates are auto-populated for Tamil Nadu regions.

3

AI Analysis

Our ML engine instantly calculates a contamination index and predicts the outbreak risk probability.

4

Alert & Act

High-risk results trigger automatic alerts. Communities are warned via SMS and WhatsApp within minutes.
